CircleLab – Starter Website Template for Learning

CircleLab is a starter template for students, educators, and math enthusiasts to explore circle geometry theorems through interactive lessons, visual proofs, and practice exercises.

Core Circle Geometry Concepts

Central Angle Theorem

The central angle measure equals its intercepted arc measure.

Inscribed Angle Theorem

An inscribed angle measures half of the arc it intercepts.

Tangent-Secant Angle

The angle between a tangent and secant equals half the difference of arcs.

Chord Properties

Equal chords subtend equal arcs and lie the same distance from the center.

Cyclic Quadrilateral

Opposite angles of a cyclic quadrilateral add up to 180°.
